WHO IS KARTHUS?

More Than A Name. A Movement.

Karthik Gupta, known by his stage name Karthus, is a hip hop artist from Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, active since 2016. His music blends lived experience with sharp perspective, reflecting maturity and a deep connection to the culture.

Karthus writes with intent and clarity, focusing on real emotions, personal struggles, growth, and life lessons. His projects Prophecy, Akele Hum, and Kissa Khatam mark different chapters of his artistic evolution, each telling stories that resonate beyond the beat.
